Personalised PillowBox / Gift Box in Kraft or White Textured Card

This listing is for Kraft Brown or White Textured Pillowboxes. These make great gift boxes for any occasion. These can be supplied blank or with personalised text.

Please read below for details on how to leave your personalisation. Please read this carefully to ensure we can provide your tags with no unnecessary delay.

Available Pack sizes are - 1, 2, 4, or 6.

Dimension are below, however please keep in mind the box curves inwards so may affect whatever you may be trying to place in it. Please check the dimensions carefully.

Specifications:

  • Colour: Kraft Brown or White Laid (Textured)
  • Length: 100mm (78mm to shortest part)
  • Width: 73mm
  • Depth: 23mm (Middle section)

PERSONALISATION INSTRUCTIONS

(PLEASE READ CAREFULLY)

  • If Personlised option selected please supply the text you require printed on your box via the message to seller section when placing your order. This will allow me to match your order with the text you require. Failing to do this may result in a delay to your order.

  • If you require a longer message printed please try keep this as short as possible, otherwise the font may look too small on the box. I have no limitation on characters, but do ask you to keep this in mind. The size of the box is detailed in the listing to help with this.
